Apply to sell at our markets

Dates for 2023 are not yet confirmed. If you want to be the first to know about our future events, sign up to our newsletter.

We welcome applications from makers that:

  • Make handmade products
  • Handmade products includes reproductions of your original work (for example surface pattern designed fabric, or illustrations printed on stationary)
  • Makers that either live or work in South East London
  • First time sellers!

Please note that at this time we are not taking any applications from produce and/or food makers. This might change in the future so please do check back with us.
