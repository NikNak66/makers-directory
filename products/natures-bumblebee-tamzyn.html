The bumblebee featured in black and white has the same level of detail of a technical drawing found in a Entomology study.

How was it made

The original version of these prints are drawn with charcoal pencils. This print edition has been printed on beautifully textured paper (known as German etching paper).

It is then placed with a mount and wrapped in cellophane before being posted.
